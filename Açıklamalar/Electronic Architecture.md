## Electronic Architecture

I used Pico 2, DC Motor, jumper cables, and L298 while doing this project.

### Connection

From Pico to L298

- GP2 with IN1
- GP3 with IN2
- GP4 with ENA
- GND with GND
- 3v3(OUT) with 5V
- External batteries with VCC
- Motor terminals with OUT 1, OUT 2
- GP5 with IN3
- GP6 with IN4
- GP7 with ENB
